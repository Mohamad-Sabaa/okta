Who We Are?

OKTA Digital is a digital marketing agency that provides unconventional marketing and sales services to B2C software companies.

Our agency was founded in June 2021 and has grown into a team of 13 pioneers who desire to build the next generation of digital marketing agencies. Since it was founded, OKTA Digital has worked with over a dozen brands owned by software companies in the United States, Europe, and Far East Asia.

Our core team and office are located in Tripoli, Lebanon; however, we also have members working with us remotely from all around Lebanon.

Who Do We Work With?

At OKTA, we believe in mastering niches rather than being a jack of all trades. Most marketing agencies in Lebanon and the Middle East want to be a “one size fits all” agency, but that is not realistic.

The type of clients we tend to work with are:

B2C SAAS companies

Focused on the retail automation space

Located in the US Europe, or Far East Asia

Generating $1 Million ARR

Ready to market the unconventional way
